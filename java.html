9/15/2005 - Billboard Hot 100's #1 song

Kanye West - Gold Digger (ft. Jamie Foxx)

I'm one of the few who prefers Late Registration over Graduation (and The College Dropout), so it's charming to see the fanfare with this song aliging with the day I started to exist. "Gold Digger" still remains one of Kanye's best-known songs, and it's a smart blend between the instrumentation present all over both his debut and sophomore albums and his more braggadocious, less-humble lyrical content he would highlight with Graduation. What I find funny is that despite Jamie Foxx's singing of the sample is what most people immediately think of when hearing of this song, "Gold Digger" would only be the second-most iconic sample he would give to hiphop. "Yo Pierre, you wanna come out here?"
